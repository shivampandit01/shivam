About Me

Hi, I’m a passionate 3D artist, visualizer and Video Editor specializing in immersive environments and cutting-edge visual content. With a strong foundation in Unreal Engine and a knack for creating dynamic 3D worlds, I thrive at the intersection of creativity and technology. My expertise spans across industry-standard software like Blender, Maya, Substance Painter, Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Photoshop, allowing me to bring both artistic vision and technical precision to every project. I have honed my skills through hands-on experience, including a 6-month video editing internship at Pro Shooter VR, where I created engaging visual narratives. I’m now applying my knowledge to push the boundaries of 3D visualization in architectural design, blending realism with imaginative storytelling.
